Butterfly Swimming

Butterfly is the most physically demanding stroke, requiring strength, flexibility, and coordination. It involves powerful arm movements and a rhythmic dolphin kick, making it ideal for building endurance and speed.

Advantages of Butterfly Swimming:

  1. Builds Strength: Works the entire body, especially shoulders and core.
  2. Improves Endurance: Requires high energy, improving stamina.
  3. Enhances Flexibility: Develops shoulder and hip mobility.
  4. Increases Speed: One of the fastest and most powerful strokes.

Butterfly Swimming Techniques

  • Body Position

    • Keep your body flat and close to the surface with a slight undulation.


  • Arm Movements

    • Use a simultaneous and powerful sweeping motion for propulsion.Maintain a smooth rhythm between arms, kick, and breathing.




  • Kicking Technique

    • Perform a strong dolphin kick, keeping legs together.




  • Breathing Technique

    • Breathe every two strokes while keeping the head low.



Butterfly Training Plan

Set Distance Focus Intensity
1 200m Warm-up (Light butterfly drills) Low
2 100m Butterfly with dolphin kick focus Medium
3 50m Butterfly sprint High
